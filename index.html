About me

I'm Lenishemarie, also known as Lola Lopez, an illustrator based in New York City. My artistic approach is dynamic and versatile, emphasizing texture while navigating through various styles. I specialize in diverse subjects with a particular focus on surreal art.
Beyond personal projects, I offer illustrations tailored for editorials and other specific needs. My work draws inspiration from life experiences, with a deliberate emphasis on themes related to mental health and fantasy.
